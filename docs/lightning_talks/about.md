## Overview of Lightning Talks Responsibilities

Work with Program team to determine time in schedule for lightning talks.

Have a way for attendees to sign up for lightning talks. We have used Google sheets for a few years. [2025's spreadsheet for reference](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1YYgrR1ERDRmfR0mkFHyuTjnCeLYdHmIc8F6_-68Mwxo/edit?gid=1515545975#gid=1515545975)
Decide if sheets should be unlocked during conference, day by day, or all at once ahead of time.
Set up a shortened link for opening slides. We've used bit.ly the last few years.

Establish rules for lightning talks for the conference. 

2025 rules:
- maximum of 5 minutes
- Preference will go to attendees who are not giving a talk or organizing.  This has been enforced loosely in 2024 and 2025 on a case by case basis.  
- Determine if a lightning talk belongs in Community Updates. In 2025, there were a few signups that better fit that section of the conference (just after Keynote each day).

Host the lightning talks (or recruit a host):
- Share the rules and expectations at the beginning of the session
- Ensure that speakers are ready early in the session
- Call speakers up to the stage, help them get ready
- Keep time and help the speaker wrap up at the end of time.
